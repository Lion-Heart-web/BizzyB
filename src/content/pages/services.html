Our Wedding Planning & Event Services

Wedding Planning

From the perfect venue to the finest details, our full-service wedding planning ensures every element is in place. We take the stress out of planning, allowing you to focus on what truly matters—celebrating your love.

  • Venue Selection:We expertly select and secure the perfect venue that suits both your style and budget.
  • Vendor Coordination:From caterers to photographers, we manage all vendor relations to ensure quality and coherence.
  • Detailed Itinerary:We provide detailed timelines and schedules, ensuring everything from the rehearsal dinner to the reception is perfectly timed.

Event Design

Transform your vision into reality with our bespoke event design. Our creative team works with you to create a cohesive aesthetic that reflects your personal style and wows your guests.

  • Theme Development:Collaborate with us to develop a unique theme that tells your story through design.

Event Coordination

Wedding Planning Packages

At Primrose Weddings & Events, we offer a variety of wedding planning packages designed to meet your specific needs. Whether you need just a little help on the day or full-service planning from start to finish, we have a package that fits.

Month-of Coordination

Ideal for those who have their plans set but need a professional to manage the day-of details.

  • In-person meeting with client 30 days prior to the event
  • Virtual meeting 7 days prior to the event to finalize details
  • Full day-of event coordination

Starting Price: Packages start at $1,500 for events with 125 guests or fewer.

Partial Planning

Perfect for couples who need assistance during the final stages of their wedding planning.

  • Three in-person meetings (30, 60, and 90 days prior to the event)
  • Day of Coordination
  • Access to our list of preferred vendors

Starting Price: 8% of the final event budget.

Full Planner Package

Best suited for couples who want comprehensive help from the very beginning of their planning process.

  • Includes everything in the Partial Planning package
  • Additional attending and coordinating vendor meetings
  • Full planning of the wedding from inception to execution
  • Assistance with booking vendors and ongoing vendor coordination

Starting Price: 14% of the final event budget.

Contact us to discuss your needs and choose the right package for your celebration. We tailor each package to your specific requirements, ensuring a perfectly planned and stress-free wedding day.
