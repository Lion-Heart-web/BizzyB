About us

Welcome to Primrose Weddings & Events

Primrose Weddings & Events is not just a company; it’s a realization of a lifelong dream for founder Briana Lopez. Rooted in the heart of Southern California, our boutique wedding planning and event management agency specializes in turning love stories into stunning celebrations.

I’m Briana, the heart and soul behind Primrose Weddings & Events. I’m thrilled you’re here—let’s dive in and get to know each other!

Let's start with a fun fact: I wasn’t the little girl dreaming about her fairytale wedding. In fact, I had no clue what I wanted until it was my turn to walk down the aisle. Instead, I discovered my passion for making others' dreams come true and decided to turn that love into my profession. And yes, I absolutely adore the movie, The Wedding Planner!

From the moment the lists start to pile up and the countdown begins, whether you’ve got everything meticulously planned or have no idea where to start, I’ll be right by your side. I’m here to turn your stress into smiles, transform your Pinterest boards into unforgettable moments, and ensure your big day is a true reflection of YOUR love story. We are based in sunny Southern California, but we love to travel (yes, take me with you!). At Primrose Weddings & Events, we specialize in turning your vision into reality, making sure every detail and moment is just as magical as you’ve dreamed.
