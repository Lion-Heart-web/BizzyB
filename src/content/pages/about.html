About us

Welcome to Primrose Weddings & Events

Primrose Weddings & Events is not just a company; it’s a realization of a lifelong dream for founder Briana Lopez. Rooted in the heart of Southern California, our boutique wedding planning and event management agency specializes in turning love stories into stunning celebrations.

We're redefining event planning and design by infusing every occasion with elegance and unmatched creativity. Let’s build your next memorable event together!

Just as every couple is unique, so is our approach to designing your perfect day. Briana started Primrose with a vision to create more than just events; she aimed to craft unforgettable experiences. With a background in event design and execution – combined with her passion for design and attention to detail – Briana and her team ensure every event is personalized and true to the client’s vision.
