Wedding Planning Packages

At Primrose Weddings & Events, we offer a variety of wedding planning packages designed to meet your specific needs. Whether you need just a little help on the day or full-service planning from start to finish, we have a package that fits.

Month-of Coordination

For the bride that has got is covered! You’re in the home stretch but need someone you can trust to get you to the finish line so you can enjoy every minute for your big day. Our Month-of Coordination service is designed to take the stress out of the final preparation. Stepping in to manage every detail in the weeks leading up to your big day. From confirming vendor arrangements to orchestrating the timeline and handling any last-minute hiccups, we ensure that everything runs smoothly so you can enjoy the magical moments of your wedding day with peace of mind.

  • Coordination beginning 12 weeks prior to event
  • Unlimited phone and email communication from coordination timeframe
  • 3 planning meetings
  • Venue site visit & walk-through
  • Timeline planning and management
  • 2 Week vendor contact handoff
  • Décor Set-Up/Tear down
  • Wedding Rehearsal, Ceremony and Reception execution
  • Vendor Liaison
  • 1 hour rehearsal
  • Up to 8 hours on day of (Additional hours $75/hour)
  • 1 assistant coordinator for up to 150 guests (Guest count over 150 will require an additional assistant for and extra fee)
